How to find your journeys data

  1. Log in to Belfast Bikes and go to your "My account" page.
  2. Highlight and copy (right-click, or Ctrl+C) the journeys text. Don't worry about including the extra text, the calculator isn't picky. Just make sure your select all of your journeys, or if you only want to calculate between certain dates, only select those journeys.
  3. Paste it into the text area on the main page.
  4. Looks good? Click Go!

About your data

Keep in mind that this is your data, and it remains as your data. The website does not capture or store your journey data (it is all handled by your web browser)
